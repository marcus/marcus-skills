Components

Every piece in the Parklet interface, from buttons that feel good to click to cards that make you want to explore the neighborhood.

01

Buttons

Buttons invite action. Primary buttons use terracotta sparingly. Ghost buttons with a trailing arrow are the most common pattern.

Primary

Limit to one primary button per visible context. If more than one action competes for attention, something has gone wrong.

Secondary

Ghost (most common)

Ghost buttons should be your default reach. They get out of the content's way while still inviting action.
